Data Model

Crises → Shelters → Resources

Three entities wired to a single auditable flow — every field carries an author, a timestamp, and a role.

Crises

The root event

Master only

Event name & type

Flood, fire, landslide

Affected area

Region, city, perimeter

Severity & status

Active · contained · closed

Lifecycle timestamps

Opened, updated, closed

Shelters

Verified nodes on the ground

Admin registers

Name, contact, address

Geo-coded, human-readable

Capacity & occupancy

How many beds, how many in

Infrastructure flags

Power, water, connectivity

Verification status

Two-minute Master approval

Resources

What each shelter needs now

Admin manages stock

General · auto-tracked

Food · water · blankets · mattresses

Specific · on demand

Medication · hygiene kits · baby items · special needs

Priority: urgent · needed · sufficient

Three roles act on this tree — Master opens crises, Admin runs shelters and stock, Leitor watches the live picture.

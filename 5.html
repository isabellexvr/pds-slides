Root Cause Analysis (Ishikawa)

Drivers of information chaos during humanitarian disaster response.

Method

No standard request/update workflow; ad-hoc steps.

Machine

Disparate tools; no dedicated system; patchy connectivity.

Measurement

No timestamps/SLA; stale or conflicting data.

Workforce

Overloaded staff/volunteers; inconsistent roles/training.

Environment

Damaged infrastructure; uncertainty; time pressure.

Materials

Vague item specs; donations without proper context.

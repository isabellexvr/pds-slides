Specific Problem

No trusted workflow for updating needs during disasters

During disasters, there's no reliable way to update and trust changing needs across multiple stakeholders.

Scattered tools, no integration

Multiple parallel spreadsheets

Different shelters maintain separate sheets with conflicting data

No validation or approval flow

Anyone can edit without verification, leading to data corruption

No real-time updates

Data becomes stale quickly during fast-moving crises

No single source of truth

Conflicting information makes decision-making impossible

Key Insight: The lack of a trusted workflow means aid doesn't reach the right place at the right time.

Current state: 3-4 days delay in updates

Target: Real-time updates

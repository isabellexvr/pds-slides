Core Insight

The problem is not the lack of donations.

It is the lack of coordination of information.

Wrong items received

Shelters receive donations that don't match their actual needs, creating waste and confusion.

Excess & shortage

Some locations have excess supplies while others face critical shortages, creating inequality.

Outdated information

Decisions are made using outdated or conflicting data, delaying critical response efforts.

Key Insight

The humanitarian sector needs a trusted workflow for updating shelter needs in real-time, moving beyond static spreadsheets to dynamic, validated information.

Source: UN OCHA Humanitarian Response Analysis, 2024
